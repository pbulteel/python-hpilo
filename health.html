Server health¶

The iLO knows a lot about your server’s physical health. The get_embedded_health() method lets you retrieve all the health information, so you can act upon it, for example in monitoring checks and management scripts. Note that the returned data can differ significantly between iLO versions.

class hpilo.Ilo
